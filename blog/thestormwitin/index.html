The wind whispers through the trees A restless sound that never cease It carries with it a sense of unease A feeling that something is not at ease

The branches sway, a dance of doubt As if they too are filled with worry The leaves rustle, a troubled shout As if they can't help but hurry

The clouds roll in, a gray despair A reflection of the heart's despair The rain falls, a tearful prayer A release from all the care

But nature is resilient, it finds a way To weather the storm and see the day It reminds us that our fears and doubts Are only temporary, they will fade away

So take a breath and look around Find solace in the sights and sounds Nature has a way of calming the mind Leaving worries and fears behind
